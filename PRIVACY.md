# Wisp Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-07-18

## 中文

Wisp（macOS 应用与配套 Chrome 扩展）**不收集、不存储、不传输任何用户数据**。

- 扩展仅在 `chatgpt.com` 页面上运行，作用只有两个：保持后台标签页的语音连接、代替用户点击页面上的语音按钮。
- 扩展与本机 Wisp 应用之间通过 Chrome Native Messaging 在本地通信，不经过任何服务器。
- 无分析统计、无广告、无第三方 SDK、无远程配置。
- 语音对话本身发生在你与 OpenAI（chatgpt.com）之间，与 Wisp 无关，适用 OpenAI 的隐私政策。

## English

Wisp (the macOS app and its companion Chrome extension) **does not collect, store, or transmit any user data**.

- The extension runs only on `chatgpt.com` and does exactly two things: keep the background tab's voice connection alive, and click the page's voice button on your behalf.
- The extension talks to the local Wisp app via Chrome Native Messaging. Nothing leaves your machine.
- No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs, no remote config.
- Your voice conversation happens directly between you and OpenAI (chatgpt.com), governed by OpenAI's privacy policy.
